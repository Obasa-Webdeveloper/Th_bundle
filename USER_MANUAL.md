# T&H BUNDLE — User Manual

## Getting Started

1. **Download the app** from the Play Store / App Store (or install the APK/TestFlight build during beta).
2. **Create an account**: tap *Sign Up*, enter your full name, email, phone number, and a password (minimum 8 characters). If someone referred you, enter their referral code to earn both of you a bonus.
3. **Verify your details**: you'll receive a 6-digit code by email and another by SMS. Enter each code on the verification screen. Codes expire after 10 minutes — tap *Resend* if needed.
4. **Log in** with your email and password. You can enable Fingerprint/Face ID afterward in *Settings → Security* for faster future logins.

## Setting Up Your Wallet

- Your wallet is created automatically when you register.
- To fund it: go to **Wallet → Fund Wallet**, enter an amount, and choose a method:
  - **Card / Bank transfer (Paystack or Flutterwave)** — you'll be redirected to a secure checkout page.
  - **Virtual Account (Monnify)** — generate a dedicated account number once, then transfer to it anytime from any bank; your wallet is credited automatically within minutes.
  - **USSD** — follow the on-screen USSD code.
- Your balance updates automatically once payment is confirmed. If it doesn't reflect within a few minutes, pull down to refresh or contact support.

## Buying Data or Airtime

1. From the Home screen, tap **Buy Data** or **Airtime**.
2. Choose your network (MTN, Airtel, Glo, or 9mobile).
3. Enter the recipient's phone number (defaults to your own).
4. Select a data plan or enter an airtime amount.
5. Tap **Buy Now**. The amount is deducted from your wallet immediately; if the purchase fails for any reason, you are automatically refunded within seconds.

## Paying Bills

- **Electricity**: select your Disco (e.g. IKEDC, EKEDC), enter your meter number, verify the customer name shown, then enter an amount and pay. Your token appears on-screen and is also saved to your transaction history.
- **Cable TV**: select DStv/GOtv/Startimes, enter your smartcard/IUC number, verify the customer name, choose a package, and pay.
- **Education**: choose WAEC, NECO, JAMB, or Result Checker to purchase a scratch card/PIN — it will be shown on-screen and saved to your history.
- **Betting**: select your platform (Bet9ja, SportyBet, etc.), enter your account/customer ID, and fund it directly from your wallet.

## Transaction History & Receipts

Go to the **History** tab to see every transaction. Tap any transaction to view details, and tap **Download Receipt** to save a PDF you can share or print.

## Referrals & Rewards

Find your unique referral code and link under **Profile → Referrals**. Share it — when someone signs up with your code and completes their first transaction, you both receive a bonus credited to your wallet. Promo codes (from ads or support) can be applied at checkout on the **Coupon** field where shown.

## Notifications

Enable push notifications when prompted so you're alerted instantly for successful purchases, wallet credits, and promos. All notifications are also stored in-app under the **Alerts** tab.

## Security Tips

- Never share your password, transaction PIN, or OTP codes with anyone — T&H BUNDLE staff will never ask for them.
- Set a transaction PIN under **Settings → Security** for an extra layer of protection on purchases.
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for maximum account security.
- If you lose access to your device, contact support immediately to freeze your wallet.

## Getting Help

- **In-app live chat**: Settings → Support → Live Chat
- **WhatsApp**: Settings → Support → WhatsApp
- **Email**: support@thbundle.com

## Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode anytime under **Settings → Appearance**.
